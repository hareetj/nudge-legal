Frequently asked questions

What is Nod?

Nod is a social discovery app for events. It uses Bluetooth to show you who's nearby and compatible with you, then helps you break the ice and meet in person.

Does Nod track my location?

No. Nod uses Bluetooth Low Energy to detect nearby users. No GPS, no maps, no location data is ever collected or stored.

Why does Nod need Bluetooth?

Bluetooth is how Nod finds people near you. Without it, the app can't detect who's in the room. You can enable it in Settings > Bluetooth.

How do I delete my account?

Open Nod, go to the Profile tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account." All your data will be permanently removed.

How do I report someone?

Tap the report button on any profile card, in a chat, or in a find channel. Select a reason and we'll review it promptly.

What happens when a chat expires?

Chats last 24 hours after a mutual nod. If the chat expires, the conversation is gone. Exchange contact info before time runs out!
